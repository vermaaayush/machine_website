About Us

Welcome to Well-Mach Engineering Systems Private Limited, a leading manufacturer of Process Equipment and Turnkey Projects Executors for the Biotech & Chemical Industries. We are committed to providing our clients with cutting-edge technology that meets their unique needs and enhances their operations.

Our team of experienced engineers, designers, and technicians work together to develop and produce machinery that is efficient, reliable, and easy to use. We use the latest manufacturing techniques and materials to ensure that our products meet the highest standards of quality and durability.

At Well-Mach, we pride ourselves on our customer-centric approach. We take the time to understand our clients' requirements and work closely with them to develop customized solutions that meet their specific needs. Our team provides comprehensive support, from initial consultation through to installation, training, and ongoing maintenance.

Our products are designed to streamline operations, increase productivity, and minimize downtime. We understand that the pharmaceutical and biotech industries are constantly evolving, and we are committed to staying ahead of the curve. We invest in research and development to ensure that our products are at the forefront of technology.

At Well-Mach, we believe in transparency, honesty, and integrity. We strive to build long-term relationships with our clients, based on mutual trust and respect. Our goal is to help our clients succeed, and we are committed to providing them with the tools they need to achieve their objectives.

Thank you for considering Well-Mach. We look forward to working with you to transform your operations and achieve your goals.

Infrastructure & Facilities

At Well-Mach Engineering Systems Private Limited, we have invested in state-of-the-art infrastructure to support our manufacturing operations and deliver cutting-edge solutions to the pharmaceutical and biotech industries. Our robust infrastructure enables us to meet the diverse needs of our clients and provide them with high-quality machinery and services

Our modern manufacturing facilities are equipped with advanced machinery, precision tools, and quality control systems. We have dedicated production areas designed to optimize efficiency, quality, and safety. Our facilities adhere to industry best practices, ensuring compliance with regulatory standards and delivering products of the highest quality.

10,000 Square Feet covered area, hHeight of 10 meters and fully back up with electric power. Our In-house manufacturing facilities consist of the following:

  • Overhead crane provision
  • TIG welding machines (110 amps to 650 amps)
  • MIG welding machines
  • Heavy duty lathe machines (6 feet to 20 feet)
  • Shaper machine
  • Auto feed radial drill machine (1.5 meter arm length and 65mm)
  • Heavy duty plate bending and rolling machine (20mm x 2000mm length)
  • Air plasma cutting machine
  • All other power and hand tools like power hacksaw, pipe cutters, hand grinders, flexible shaft grinders for SS polishing.
  • Our skilled and dedicated manpower are updated with our latest Product Developments
  • Standardized our vendors (Dishend Bending, CNC Plasma - Bending and Machining, Laser and Orbital welding etc) nearby only for best quality and intime monitoring.
  • Every equipment is called by “Third Party Inspections at regular intervals” and are supported with ethnical documents / certificates like material test certificates, DQ / IQ / OQ/, Destructive / non-destructive testing (hydro, pneumatic, radiography, UT, MPI etc)
